About Andrew Yacoub

I'm a dedicated and versatile software engineer with 4+ years of experience, specializing in building scalable web applications and architecting backend systems. My journey started in Mechatronics, but my passion for problem-solving led me to transition into software development—where I found my true calling.

My core expertise lies in Ruby on Rails and Python, with strong proficiency in FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, RabbitMQ, and Elasticsearch. I've worked on diverse projects, from real-time chat systems and e-learning platforms to enterprise-scale engagement and analytics products. I'm also experienced with Docker, AWS, and modern DevOps practices.

I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and thrive in collaborative environments. I'm currently focused on mastering system design, data structures & algorithms, and contributing to impactful backend architecture—aiming to join a top-tier tech company.

Outside of coding, I enjoy mentoring others, creating educational content (like my upcoming Udemy course on DSA in Ruby), and exploring the intersection of software engineering and personal growth.
